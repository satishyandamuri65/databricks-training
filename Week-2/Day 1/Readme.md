# Week 2 - Day 1 - College Management System

## Overview

Day 1 of Week 2 focused on practising SQL on a new schema — a College Management System (CMS). The dataset has 5 tables: Department, Staff, Student, Subject and Mark. Practiced joins, aggregations, subqueries and filtering on real-world style college data.

---

## Topics Covered

- Left Join to include unmatched rows (staff without departments, students without marks)
- Inner Join across multiple tables
- Finding unmatched rows using `IS NULL`
- Group By with aggregate functions (avg, count, sum, max, min)
- Having clause to filter grouped results
- Subqueries for average comparisons
- Correlated subqueries for per-group comparisons
- Order By with CGPA ranking
- Filtering with multiple conditions using AND
